# CDONHS-SHS Home Dashboard Content Plan

## 1) Introduction Section (Hero)

**Title**  
Cagayan de Oro National High School - Senior High School

**Subtitle**  
Official online hub for announcements, enrollment updates, and academic information.

**Intro Paragraph**  
Welcome to the CDONHS-SHS dashboard. This page gives students, teachers, and administrators quick access to official announcements, enrollment updates, school programs, and important reminders from the school.

**Primary Button**  
View Official Facebook Announcements

**Secondary Button**  
Explore School Information

---

## 2) Recommended Dashboard Blocks

1. Announcements (top priority)  
Use a Facebook link/button and show 3 latest post titles manually updated by admin.

2. Mission and Vision  
Use DepEd mission/vision text in two side-by-side cards.

3. Enrollment Updates  
Show current school year, semester, and key enrollment reminders.

4. Featured School Photos  
Use a 3-5 image gallery from the `Assets/dashboard_samples` folder.

5. Quick Links  
Role-specific links (already in hamburger menu), plus optional "Contact School" and "Download Forms".
